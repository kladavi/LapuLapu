## Executive Summary

**Frictionless Customer Experience:** Employee Experience Dashboard coverage advanced to 67 of 81 systems used by Japan users, and the Japan Business Capability dashboard moved into leadership-review readiness with clearer alert labeling now tracked. A new operating decision also set the rule that non-standard monitoring gaps will be escalated to owning teams rather than absorbed through ad hoc workarounds.

**Robust Technical Core:** PS-to-GOCC transition progressed to 22 of 65 applications fully transitioned, with 3 still in progress, and the team formalized the next control layer around alert workflows, non-production response paths, rapid recovery reboot and restore procedures, and a reusable transition scaffolding pack. An SRM incident review also established that production support needs explicit validation, current runbooks, and a clear restart or recovery path, with SRM now pulled forward as a focused Rapid Recovery onboarding case while RCA continues on the underlying cause.

**Outstanding Colleague Experience:** Dashboard review artifacts and the standardized handover taxonomy improved clarity for leadership, GOCC, and ETS Japan teams, reducing ambiguity in how additional applications will be onboarded and supported.
